Change screen resolution in Linux 
xrandr method
10/23/2018
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Usage: Allows to add and change resolution unsupported in xrandr for specific monitors. 
Needs 3 parameters in the following format(width height refreshRate)

Example: ./screen.sh 1920 1080 60

Standard 16:9 resolutions: 1024×576, 1152×648, 1280×720, 1366×768, 1600×900, 1920×1080, 2560×1440 and 3840×2160.
